Privacy Policy

Application: FilvockCutsApp  |  Last updated: April 6, 2026  |  Website: https://filvock.github.io/FilvockCutsApp/

This Privacy Policy explains how FilvockCutsApp ("the Application", "the App", "we") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our service. By using FilvockCutsApp, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. About FilvockCutsApp

FilvockCutsApp is a personal desktop application that processes local video files and publishes short-form clips to TikTok via TikTok's Content Posting API. The App runs entirely on the user's local machine and does not operate any external servers.

2. Information We Collect

FilvockCutsApp collects only the minimum data necessary to operate. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server operated by FilvockCutsApp.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

FilvockCutsApp does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identification information
  • Passwords or login credentials for any platform
  • Usage analytics, telemetry, or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Any data from third parties or other users

FilvockCutsApp does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of advertising technology.

4. How We Use Your Information

The data collected by FilvockCutsApp is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain an active session
  • Upload and publish video clips to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • Display your connected account information within the App's dashboard

5. TikTok API and Third-Party Data Practices

FilvockCutsApp uses TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API. When you authenticate, TikTok may collect and process data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. FilvockCutsApp has no control over TikTok's data practices and encourages users to review TikTok's policies directly.

FilvockCutsApp is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to TikTok or ByteDance Ltd.

6. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data (API tokens, credentials) used by FilvockCutsApp is stored in a local configuration file (tiktok_config.json) on your device. This file is not encrypted by default; users are responsible for maintaining the security of their device and file system.

No data is transmitted to any external server operated by FilvockCutsApp.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

FilvockCutsApp retains data only for as long as you use the App. You can delete all data at any time by removing the tiktok_config.json configuration file from the App's directory. This will disconnect your TikTok account and erase all stored tokens.

To revoke FilvockCutsApp's access to your TikTok account, you can also visit your TikTok account settings and remove the app's authorization.

8. Children's Privacy

FilvockCutsApp is not directed at or intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us immediately.
