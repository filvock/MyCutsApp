Connect your TikTok account

Authorize FilvockCutsApp to publish short video clips to your TikTok account automatically from your local dashboard.

Continue with TikTok
Permissions requested
👤
user.info.basic
Read your public profile to confirm account identity
📤
video.upload
Upload video files to TikTok servers
✅
video.publish
Publish uploaded videos as posts on your behalf

How it works

1 Click "Continue with TikTok" — you will be redirected to TikTok's authorization page
2 Review and approve the requested permissions on TikTok's secure page
3 You are redirected back and your account is connected to FilvockCutsApp
4 Use the local dashboard to queue and publish clips to your account

By connecting your account you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. FilvockCutsApp will only post content you explicitly queue for publishing. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.
